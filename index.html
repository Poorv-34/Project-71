What's New

  • Online Classes

    If your child does not want to attend school in-person, you can enroll in online classes. The cirriculum will be the same as well as the schedule, just in an online matter. Students may use meeting applications such as Google Meet and Zoom.

  • State Exam

    1. Purpose The Forward Exam results provide information about student performance which allows students to reflect on their achievement, teachers to target instruction to student needs and reflect upon their own instructional practices, and administrators to more fully understand what students know and can do in order to guide curriculum and professional development decisions.
    2.Scores Scores of the exam are split into 4 categories: Advanced, Proficient, Basic, and Below Basic (Written from best to worst). Scores are ranked based on how good you did.
    3. Testing Areas Students will be testing using our school computers at the computer lab. It is also optional to bring your own chromebook or computer to test. Online Students will be also be going to the school.
